Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

1. Never share your UPI PIN with anyone.
2. Do not approve unknown payment requests.
3. Avoid clicking on suspicious links or messages.
4. Always verify the identity of callers claiming to be from banks.
5. Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts.
6. Enable two-factor authentication wherever possible.
7. Keep your apps and devices updated regularly.
8. Do not install apps from unknown sources.

UPI Safety Tips:
9. Remember that receiving money does not require entering your UPI PIN.
10. Always check the receiver’s details before making payments.

Report cybercrime immediately:
* National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
* Helpline Number: 1930
